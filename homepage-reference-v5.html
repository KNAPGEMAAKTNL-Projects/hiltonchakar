A letter from the host

We opened the doors because we couldn't stop coming back. Now you can too.

Hilton Chakar is three apartments in the medina of Tangier, restored room by room over the last few years. We didn't set out to run a hotel. We set out to make three places we'd want to wake up in ourselves — a small kitchen, a terrace that catches the afternoon, a doorway you'd open every morning without a keycard.

We host short stays and long ones. We reply to WhatsApp at any reasonable hour, the bread bakery is two streets away, and we will tell you exactly which café not to bother with.

Yours,

How long are you here for?
