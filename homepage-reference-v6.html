A note, before you arrive

Not a hotel room. Your own apartment, high in the Hilton Tangier, with a kitchen, a terrace and Tangier laid out below. We keep the three of them ourselves, the way we'd want to find them: light, quiet, shutters open to the afternoon.

There's no front desk and no concierge to get past. You let yourself in. We're a WhatsApp message away, and we'll usually answer the same evening. Short stay or a couple of months, the apartment is yours while you're here.

The booking, in practice

Most stays are confirmed by message, not by form.

WhatsApp is how Morocco works, and how we work too. Send us a few words about your dates. We'll usually answer the same evening, with availability, a price, and a couple of recent photos of whichever door fits.

If the form below feels more comfortable, that works too. Either reaches a human.
